Scientists   have   developed   several   models   to   forecast   this   point ,   and   some   put   the   date   at   2020   or   later .   One   of   the   most   famous   forecast   models   is   called   the   Hubbert   model2 .   It   assumes   that   global   oil   production   will   follow   a   bell   shaped   curve3 . The   scientists   also   showed   that   the   world ' s   oil   reserves7   are   being   reduced   at   a   rate   of   2.1   percent   a   year .
